Welcome Queen Family

This site is an ever-evolving resource to provide the most comprehensive and up-to-date information on Queen and its four main components: John Deacon, Brian May, Freddie Mercury, and Roger Taylor. Within the pages you will find detailed information on all band and solo releases – albums, singles, and videos – as well as other relevant odds and ends – BBC sessions, interviews, the musical, and so forth. Every album, single and song have their own page, detailing release dates, chart positions and much more. Queenpedia is run by a dedicated group of fans, who will ensure that all of the information contained within is as correct and updated as it can be. Come back soon, and come back often, but most importantly, enjoy!
